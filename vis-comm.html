Petra Project

This project was created in collaboration with Professor of Archaeology Felipe Rojas from Brown University and Professor of Anthropology Sarah Newman at the University of Chicago based on their current archeological research in Petra, Jordan. I worked with one partner to brainstorm and illustrate this piece which will be used for the professors educational and research purposes.

Project Brief

The visual that the researchers needed for this site would show that the various features of the system are interconnected by a complex water system and other elements, such as cisterns, baetyls, and a sluice. The target illustrations should be a combination of plans and sections and perspectival drawings in order to convey the system and the scale of the cliff.

Project Focus:

UI/UX design, product design, collaboration

Tools:

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Procreate
