Overview

As the design lead for Hack@Brown, a student organization at Brown University that organizes and hosts an annual hackathon for college students all around the country, I worked to lead a team to design the website and brand identity of the event.

The theme this year was "Space", so our website and branding is all inspired by this concept.

Project Focus:

Web design

Design Team:

Alex Hogue, Vivian Li, Kathryn Lee, Sherry Zhang, Cynthia Wang, Emily Lin, Michael Ma

Tools:

Figma, Blender

Preperation

We formed a list of words that came to mind when thinking of "space":

  • planets (animation– rotating planets)
  • stars
  • UFO
  • astronaut (astronaut bear)
  • alien (alien bear)
  • rocket ships
  • sun & moon
  • telescope
  • satellites

Design Choices

Taking inspiration from our mood board, we wanted to create a futuristic and vibrant style using a unique font for headings and vibrant accent colors.
